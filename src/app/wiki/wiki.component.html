Engineering Wiki

Document research notes, design calculations, and handy reference tables.

Treat this area like a living notebook. Short entries with diagrams and data tables are ideal. Consider linking out to Google Drive, Notion, or Confluence pages if you collaborate with others.

  • Design standards: tolerances, fastener charts, material selections.
  • Process documentation: assembly guides, testing procedures, commissioning checklists.
  • Learning log: quick tips picked up while experimenting with new tools.

When you are ready to persist entries, add a data service under src/app/core or connect to MongoDB. The architecture already separates UI components from data so the transition is straightforward.
